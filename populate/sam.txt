Recipe Name: Mushroom Risotto
    Servings: 4
    Ingredients: Arborio rice, mushrooms, onion, garlic, vegetable broth, Parmesan cheese, butter, white wine, parsley
    Tags: Italian, vegetarian, creamy
    Short Description: Creamy mushroom risotto with rich flavors.
    Detailed Description:

    Sauté onion and garlic in butter.
    Add Arborio rice and cook until translucent.
    Pour in white wine and stir until absorbed.
    Add vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly.
    Stir in mushrooms and cook until tender.
    Mix in Parmesan cheese.
    Garnish with parsley.
    Serve hot and enjoy!
Recipe Name: Chicken Parmesan
    Servings: 4
    Ingredients: Chicken breast, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, marinara sauce, flour, eggs, olive oil
    Tags: Italian, baked, dinner
    Short Description: Crispy chicken Parmesan with marinara sauce.
    Detailed Description:

    Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    Bread chicken breasts with flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan.
    Fry chicken in olive oil until golden.
    Place chicken in a baking dish, top with marinara sauce and mozzarella.
    Bake for 20 minutes.
    Serve hot.
    Enjoy your crispy chicken!
    Perfect for dinner.
Recipe Name: Veggie Burger
    Servings: 4
    Ingredients: Black beans, breadcrumbs, onion, garlic, cumin, paprika, egg, burger buns, lettuce, tomato, avocado
    Tags: Vegetarian, healthy, easy
    Short Description: Healthy and tasty veggie burger.
    Detailed Description:

    Mash black beans in a bowl.
    Mix in breadcrumbs, chopped onion, garlic, cumin, paprika, and egg.
    Form into patties.
    Cook patties in a skillet until browned.
    Serve on burger buns with lettuce, tomato, and avocado.
    Enjoy your veggie burger!
    Perfect for a healthy meal.

Recipe Name: Apple Pie
    Servings: 8
    Ingredients: Apples, flour, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, butter, pie crust
    Tags: Dessert, baking, pie
    Short Description: Classic apple pie with a flaky crust.
    Detailed Description:

    Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    Peel and slice apples.
    Mix apples with sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
    Place apples in pie crust.
    Dot with butter and cover with top crust.
    Bake for 50 minutes.
    Let cool before serving.
    Enjoy your apple pie!
Recipe Name: Lentil Soup
    Servings: 4
    Ingredients: Lentils, carrot, celery, onion, garlic, tomato paste, vegetable broth, cumin, salt, pepper
    Tags: Soup, vegetarian, healthy
    Short Description: Hearty and healthy lentil soup.
    Detailed Description:

    Sauté onion, garlic, carrot, and celery in a pot.
    Stir in tomato paste and cumin.
    Add lentils and vegetable broth.
    Simmer until lentils are tender.
    Season with salt and pepper.
    Serve hot.
    Enjoy your hearty soup!
    Perfect for a light meal.
