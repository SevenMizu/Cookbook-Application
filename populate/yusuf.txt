Recipe Name: Fish Tacos
    Servings: 4
    Ingredients: White fish fillets, taco seasoning, taco shells, cabbage, lime, cilantro, sour cream
    Tags: Mexican, seafood, quick
    Short Description: Light and flavorful fish tacos.
    Detailed Description:

    Season fish fillets with taco seasoning.
    Grill or fry the fish until cooked through.
    Warm taco shells.
    Fill taco shells with fish, cabbage, and cilantro.
    Squeeze lime juice over tacos.
    Add a dollop of sour cream.
    Serve immediately.
    Enjoy your seafood tacos!
Recipe Name: Pancakes
    Servings: 4
    Ingredients: Flour, milk, eggs, baking powder, sugar, salt, butter, maple syrup
    Tags: Breakfast, easy, quick
    Short Description: Fluffy homemade pancakes.
    Detailed Description:

    In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
    Beat in milk and eggs.
    Melt butter in a skillet.
    Pour batter onto skillet to form pancakes.
    Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
    Serve with butter and maple syrup.
    Enjoy your fluffy pancakes!
    Perfect for breakfast.
Recipe Name: Shrimp Scampi
    Servings: 4
    Ingredients: Shrimp, garlic, butter, lemon juice, parsley, spaghetti, olive oil
    Tags: Seafood, pasta, quick
    Short Description: Quick and easy shrimp scampi pasta.
    Detailed Description:

    Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
    In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic.
    Add shrimp and cook until pink.
    Stir in lemon juice and parsley.
    Toss shrimp with spaghetti.
    Drizzle with olive oil.
    Serve immediately.
    Enjoy your seafood pasta!
Recipe Name: Chocolate Cake
    Servings: 8
    Ingredients: Flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, eggs, milk, butter, vanilla extract
    Tags: Dessert, baking, cake
    Short Description: Rich and moist chocolate cake.
    Detailed Description:

    Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
    Beat in eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
    Pour batter into a greased cake pan.
    Bake for 30-35 minutes.
    Let cool before serving.
    Enjoy your chocolate cake!
    Perfect for dessert.
Recipe Name: Chicken Curry
    Servings: 4
    Ingredients: Chicken breast, curry powder, coconut milk, onion, garlic, ginger, tomatoes, cilantro
    Tags: Indian, spicy, dinner
    Short Description: Spicy and flavorful chicken curry.
    Detailed Description:

    Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger in a pot.
    Add chicken and curry powder, cook until browned.
    Pour in coconut milk and tomatoes.
    Simmer until chicken is cooked through.
    Garnish with cilantro.
    Serve with rice.
    Enjoy your spicy curry!
    Perfect for dinner.
