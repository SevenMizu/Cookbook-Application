Recipe Name: Lasagna
    Servings: 6
    Ingredients: Ground beef, lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, tomato sauce, garlic, onion, Italian seasoning
    Tags: Italian, pasta, baked
    Short Description: Classic layered lasagna with rich flavors.
    Detailed Description:

    Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
    Sauté garlic and onion, add ground beef and cook until browned.
    Stir in tomato sauce and Italian seasoning.
    In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses.
    Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
    Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
    Let cool slightly before serving.
    Enjoy your hearty lasagna!
Recipe Name: French Toast
    Servings: 2
    Ingredients: Bread, eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, butter, maple syrup
    Tags: Breakfast, easy, sweet
    Short Description: Sweet and fluffy French toast.
    Detailed Description:

    Whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla extract in a bowl.
    Dip bread slices in the mixture.
    Melt butter in a skillet.
    Cook bread slices until golden brown on both sides.
    Serve with maple syrup.
    Enjoy your sweet breakfast!
    Perfect for a weekend morning.
    Add fruit toppings if desired.
Recipe Name: Baked Salmon
    Servings: 4
    Ingredients: Salmon fillets, lemon, dill, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper
    Tags: Seafood, healthy, baked
    Short Description: Healthy and flavorful baked salmon.
    Detailed Description:

    Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet.
    Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
    Sprinkle with dill, garlic, salt, and pepper.
    Bake for 20 minutes.
    Serve with lemon wedges.
    Enjoy your healthy meal!
    Perfect for dinner.
Recipe Name: Beef Stew
    Servings: 6
    Ingredients: Beef chunks, potatoes, carrots, onions, beef broth, garlic, thyme, salt, pepper
    Tags: Dinner, hearty, comfort food
    Short Description: Hearty and comforting beef stew.
    Detailed Description:

    Brown beef chunks in a pot.
    Add garlic and onions, cook until softened.
    Pour in beef broth and add potatoes and carrots.
    Season with thyme, salt, and pepper.
    Simmer until beef is tender.
    Serve hot.
    Enjoy your comforting stew!
    Perfect for a cold night.
Recipe Name: Caesar Salad
    Servings: 2
    Ingredients: Romaine lettuce, Caesar dressing, croutons, Parmesan cheese, lemon juice, black pepper
    Tags: Salad, easy, classic
    Short Description: Classic Caesar salad with a tangy dressing.
    Detailed Description:

    Chop Romaine lettuce and place in a bowl.
    Add Caesar dressing and toss to coat.
    Sprinkle with croutons and Parmesan cheese.
    Squeeze lemon juice over salad.
    Season with black pepper.
    Serve immediately.
    Enjoy your tangy salad!
    Perfect for a light meal.
